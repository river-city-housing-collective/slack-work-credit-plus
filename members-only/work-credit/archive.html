Hi! This is a secret page for admins only. If you don't have write access to the Airtable then it probably won't be of much interest to you. In order to skirt around the free limits of Airtable, you may occassionally need to collapse Reported Time records into "Balance Forward" totals. This page will make that process quick and painless.

  1. In Airtable, under the Reported Time table in your Work Credit Tracking base, click the "..." icon and then "Download CSV". Make sure you're getting the main Grid View with no filters on! This will provide you with a backup of the existing Reported Time records.
  2. Click the top corner of the table below to select all cells and then press Ctrl+C to copy them all.
  3. Back in Airtable, select all records in the Reported Time table (again, make sure it's actually ALL and not a filtered subset), right click, and select "Delete all selected records"
  4. In the top-left most cell, paste (Ctrl+V) the copied data into the Reported Time table. Done!
